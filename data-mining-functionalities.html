Data Mining Functionalities

What can Data Mining actually DO?

Two main categories of tasks:

üîçDESCRIPTIVE

What happened? What patterns exist?

üîÆPREDICTIVE

Descriptive Tasks: Understanding the Past

üîçThree Main Types:

  • Classification: Sorting things into categories
  • Clustering: Finding hidden groups
  • Association Rules: Discovering relationships
Think about it: When you organize your music playlist, photos, or files - you're doing descriptive data mining manually!

Classification: The Sorting Expert

üìãSupervised Learning

Goal: Learn from examples to classify new data

Real Examples:

  • Email: Spam vs. Not Spam
  • Medical: Tumor ‚Üí Benign vs. Malignant
  • Banking: Loan ‚Üí Approve vs. Reject
  • Education: Student ‚Üí Pass vs. Fail
Quick Question: What other classification examples can you think of?

Clustering: The Group Finder

üéØUnsupervised Learning

Goal: Find hidden patterns without knowing the answer

Real Examples:

  • Netflix: Group users by viewing preferences
  • Marketing: Customer segments for targeting
  • Social Media: Community detection
  • Genetics: Group genes by similar functions
Think-Pair-Share: How might Instagram use clustering? (30 seconds with neighbor)

Association Rules: The Connection Detective

üõíMarket Basket Analysis

Goal: Find items that frequently appear together

Famous Examples:

  • Supermarket: "People who buy bread also buy butter"
  • Amazon: "Customers who bought X also bought Y"
  • Netflix: "People who watched A also watched B"
  • Spotify: "Songs often played together"
Fun Fact: The classic example is "Beer & Diapers" - discovered that men buying diapers often bought beer too!

Predictive Tasks: Glimpsing the Future

üîÆTwo Main Types:

  • Regression: Predicting numbers (How much? How many?)
  • Time Series: Predicting future trends
Key Difference: Descriptive looks at existing data, Predictive forecasts future data

Regression: The Number Prophet

üìàNumerical Prediction

Goal: Predict a specific number value

Real Examples:

  • Real Estate: Predict house prices
  • Sales: Predict quarterly revenue
  • Education: Predict student GPA
  • Weather: Predict tomorrow's temperature
Quick Poll: Raise your hand if you've ever tried to predict your exam score before getting results!

Time Series: The Trend Tracker

üìäForecasting Over Time

Goal: Predict future values based on historical trends

Real Examples:

  • Stock Market: Predict tomorrow's prices
  • Weather: 7-day forecast
  • Business: Monthly sales projections
  • Social Media: Trending topics prediction
